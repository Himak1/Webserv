CODAM DURING COVID-19

Latest update: 2021-09-17

The health and safety of our students is our biggest priority. We are constantly monitoring government guidelines and adapting to them. Changes can always occur as we will adapt when necessary. Keep an eye on this FAQ or on our social media, where we will share new information

Visitors, students and employees at Codam must follow the following guidelines from the 20th of september 2021, and onwards:

Dutch Corona Check app
From the 20th of September, 2021, whether you are a student, a staff member, or a guest, access to the building will be constrained by showing your ID, and a valid QR code readable by the official Dutch Corona Check app at the gate each day.
The guard will check your ID and QR code with the official Corona Check scanner. If it shows a green checkmark, they will unblock your card until 07:00 the next day.

What can we see in the Corona Check app?
The scanner turns green if you have:

  • (a) Valid negative test of less than 24 hours, This test must take place at one of the affiliated test locations: Https://www.testenvoortoegang.org;
  • (b) Recover;
  • (c) Vaccination certificate.

    • We cannot see if it is a valid negative test, recovery, or vaccination certificate.

    Vaccinated abroad?
    Please visit the official website of the Dutch Government to Get proof of vaccination after being vaccinated outside the Netherlands.

    Can I still apply to Codam?
    Yes, absolutely. A part of your application process has always been online. Being at home right now might be a good moment to do the online test considering it takes 2 hours and 10 minutes in total. We encourage you to start the online test, and enroll in a check-in right away!

    Are you still organizing check-ins?
    Yes, we do! We are organizing onsite check-ins again from the 20th of September, 2021, and onwards. Please bring along your ID card and your CoronaCheckApp to the Check-in. You will be asked about it at the entrance.

    Whenever you pass the test the available spots for a check-in will become visible on apply.codam.nl. From there on you can book a spot. All the necessary information on how to attend such a meeting will be sent your way after booking.
