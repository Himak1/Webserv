Cookie Statement Codam Coding College

We store general visitor statistics on our website to analyze our online performance and improve the functionalities. Some cookies are placed by third parties that work on our behalf.

Our use of cookies and similar technologies

  • Sign up and authentication: we use cookies to verify you. When you sign up to Codam, we store a unique ID number in an encrypted cookie on your device. By using this cookie you can go from page to page within the website, without having to re-register on every page. You can also save your login information so that you do not have to log in every time you return to the site.
  • Social media: Codam works with social media cookies, so that we can retarget you about features of Codam that might interest you.
  • YouTube: we use third party cookies from YouTube, so that we can show you relevant videos about Codam or any other videos that might be interesting for you.
  • Analytics: we use Google Analytics and the heatmaps of Hotjar to gather usage and performance data of our website. This way, we can update our website to your needs. We use Google Analytics cookies to, for example, count the number of unique visitors to our website. We use Hotjar to, for example, see how users are moving throughout our website. All of this data is anonymized data.
  • Interaction: we use third party cookies from Collect.chat to make sure we can be of assistance wherever you are on the website. You can chat with us through this tool.

Some of the cookies that we use a lot are listed below. The list is not exhaustive, but is intended to illustrate the primary purposes for which we generally set cookies. When you visit Codam, the site places some or all cookies from the list below:

  • APISID, HSID, PREF, SAPISID, SSID: for targeting and advertising with Google Adwords _ga, _gid: for performance and analytics
  • LOGIN_INFO: to keep you logged in
  • SID: for session state management
  • YSC: this cookie is set by the YouTube video service on pages with embedded YouTube video
  • __cfduid: used to speed up page load times
